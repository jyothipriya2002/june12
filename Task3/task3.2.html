Chhota Bheem

Season1
Episode1

The series is set in a village in the kingdom of Dholakpur. The series revolves around Bheem, sometimes referred to as Chhota Bheem due to his young age, a boy who is strong and intelligent.Bheem is a well-loved figure in the village, known for his good heart and helping hand. His closest friends are Chutki, a clever girl; Raju, a brave toddler wearing nothing but his underpants; and a talking monkey called Jaggu.
